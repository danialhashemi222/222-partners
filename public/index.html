we exist to bring people together in person.

222 hosts curated experiences every day of the week. members sign up, answer a detailed personality questionnaire, and are immediately invited to upcoming events with groups of strangers matched to them. no photos, no swiping, just showing up.

a typical 222 experience includes dinner in groups of 6, followed by a second, larger venue where between 3 and 20 of these groups come together. from your perspective, our members are just normal customers coming to your location.
