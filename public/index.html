common questions

before we get on a call.

how much does it cost?

+

for venues and restaurants: $3–5 per member sent. we only charge for members that checked in via in-app location tracking. if you're ever dissatisfied, we refund it. for curators: you set the price, we sell the tickets, you keep 90%.

do i need to change how i operate?

+

not at all. our members are just normal customers. for venues, we just need a dedicated area for the group on the night. for curators, you submit experiences through your dashboard and we handle everything else.

what if i'm not happy with the group?

+

we'll refund your fee — no questions asked. but our members are young professionals who show up to spend and have a good time. they're the kind of customers you want.

how do members find my venue?

+

we handle all the matchmaking and logistics. members get invited to experiences at your venue through the 222 app — they don't search for you, we send them to you.

what kind of feedback do i get?

+

after every experience, you get a private dashboard with summarized highlights and pain points, demographic insights, and individual comments from guests. 87% of attendees leave feedback.

how often will you send people?

+

that's up to you. you pick the dates and frequency. most partners start with once or twice a month and increase from there.

what makes 222 different from a promotion group?

+

222 is a technology company. members download our app and take a 15-minute questionnaire where they tell us about their tastes and interests. we use that information to send them to venues we know they'd like. for you, this means that the members we send your way are more likely to enjoy your venue or event and therefore become a repeat customer.
